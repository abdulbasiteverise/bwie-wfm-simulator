BWIE is a Discrete Event Simulation (DES) engine built to model real contact center operations beyond traditional Erlang formulas. Instead of assuming infinite customer patience, the system simulates customer abandonment behavior, staffing variability, shrinkage, skill routing, and intraday volatility to produce realistic operational outcomes.
