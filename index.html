How BWIE Works

BWIE simulates contact center performance across 15-minute intervals using queue dynamics. It models the interaction between incoming call demand, agent staffing levels, average handle time, customer patience and shrinkage to estimate service performance.

Each interval calculates the effective staffing capacity, queue buildup, expected wait time and resulting service level. Unlike traditional Erlang models, this approach approximates real operational behavior by allowing queues and workload to propagate across intervals.
